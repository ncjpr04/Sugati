AI Assistance Layer

Intelligent. Optional. Labelled.

All AI features in Sugati are assistive — never automatic. Every suggestion is clearly labelled ✦. The specialist reviews and approves before anything leaves the platform.

✦
AI reads from live Salesforce data — it never invents trip details.
Compose with AI, Rewrite, Suggested Recipients, and Suggested Attachments all pull from the Opportunity record, its relationships, and communication history. The specialist always has full editorial control.
✍
✦ Assistive
Compose with AI

Describe the message in plain English. AI generates a personalised draft with merge tokens pre-inserted, adapted to the selected channel (rich text for Email, plain text for WhatsApp, structured for In-App).

✦ Reading from
Opportunity · Supplier Bookings · Travelling Countries · Group Members · Communication History
🔄
✦ Assistive
Rewrite with AI

Select any passage in the composer. AI offers three rewrite modes. Original text is preserved as one undo step. Tone adapts to channel — formal for Email suppliers, warm and brief for WhatsApp.

LWC Component Architecture

Component Tree
OpportunityCommunicationHub
LWC · Container
Root component. Hosts channel summary, communication timeline, and compose trigger.
ChannelSelector
LWC · Modal
Three-card channel picker. Routes to Email, WhatsApp, or In-App composer.
EmailComposer
LWC · Composer
Tiptap editor, addressing, subject, toolbar. Full rich-text email composition.
WhatsAppComposer
LWC · Composer
Template selector, plain-text area, positional merge tokens, media attachments.
InAppComposer
LWC · Composer
Notification type, title, body, document link. Minimal structured form.
RecipientSelector
LWC · Shared
Audience type cards. Travellers, agents, suppliers, contacts. Per-row selection.
MergeFieldPicker
LWC · Panel
Object group list + field token grid. Inserts at Tiptap cursor or WA position.
AttachmentManager
LWC · Panel
Three sections: Opportunity files, Country files, Dynamic documents. Email only.
PreviewPanel
LWC · Preview
Per-channel rendered preview. Switches between Email, WhatsApp bubble, In-App card.
CommunicationHistory
LWC · Timeline
Omnichannel timeline with channel tags, status pills, timestamps. Linked to Comm_Log__c.
